# List of gameplay features

* Hybrid fight system
  * Turn based fight
  * Real-time fight
* Variety of views
  * Isometric view
  * Top view (tactical)
  * TPV
  * FPV
* Additional modes
  * Simulator: car driving etc.
  * Air battles (inspired by "The Saga of Tanya the Evil")
  * Economic strategy (developing the infrastructure of our clan)
